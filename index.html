I'm not a Robot But I sure love them!

I am currently a Master's student at the University of Brasilia (UnB) working at the Laboratory of Robotics and Automation (LARA). I have a wide range of interests: I'm a robotics enthusiat, life enthusiast and netflix enthusiat.

In the academic aspect of my life, I'm currently investigating topics such as bimanual manipulation and force description for serial manipulators within the algebra of dual quaternions. However, I'm also interested in topics such as computer vision and different ways we can control and interact with humanoid robots.
